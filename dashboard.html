📊 Key Insights: Why 5G-V2X Outperforms

⚡ Ultra-Low Latency: 5G-V2X achieves ~1ms latency compared to DSRC's 4ms and C-V2X's 20ms, critical for safety applications like collision avoidance.
📡 Extended Range: With 1000m range, 5G-V2X covers 3x more distance than DSRC (300m) and 2x more than C-V2X (500m).
🚀 Higher Bandwidth: 1000 Mbps bandwidth enables rich data exchange including HD maps, sensor fusion, and real-time video streaming.
✅ Superior Reliability: 99% reliability ensures critical safety messages are delivered even in challenging conditions.
🏙️ Density Performance: Advanced beamforming and interference management allow 5G-V2X to maintain performance in high-density urban scenarios.
🏎️ High-Speed Mobility: Better Doppler effect compensation enables reliable communication at highway speeds (120+ km/h).
